jWAY

Fun to the Last HOP!™

A THRILLING new twist on traditional hopscotch where players compete to claim spaces on the board, whoever has the most claimed spaces by the time the board fills up WINS!

How To Play

jWAY is a quick multiplayer game where players jump strategically though the board and claim spots until the entire board is filled.

By following jWAY's "Things To Know" simple instructional guidelines, players can easily learn the rules and play the game.

Benefits

jWAY is proven to be the perfect addition to any physical education department, after school recreation and/or recreational camp. It provides healthy exercise, improved coordination/motor skills, strategy amongst players and friendly competition without physical contact. We believe the best way to be healthy and active is to have fun at the same time.
